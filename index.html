About Rabindranath Tagore

Early Life & Education

Born in 1861 in Kolkata, Tagore’s eclectic education through home tutors and brief schooling shaped his boundless creativity.

Literary Contributions

Tagore’s _Gitanjali_ won him the 1913 Nobel Prize, the first for a non-European, with profound poetry, novels, and essays.

Indian Renaissance

A key figure in the Bengal Renaissance, Tagore championed art, education, and social reform, inspiring Indian independence.
